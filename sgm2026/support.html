Four ways to help

20 days since petition — acknowledged each time, none acted on.

01
Add your name

The petition has been submitted — now we are building a wider base of support to increase pressure on the committee. Every additional member who formally registers makes the committee's position harder to hold. Add your name, membership number, and a note saying "SGM support". Your details will not be shared without your consent.

Add your name →
02
Attend the SGM

Fifty members are required for a quorum. Your physical presence at the meeting is one of the most important things you can do. When the date is announced, please make every effort to attend — and encourage fellow members to do the same.

Only acceptable: 23 May · Notice by 9 May

Register above and we will notify you the moment the committee confirms the date. The window is closing.

03
Share this site with fellow members

Many members — especially in the diaspora — are unaware of what has been happening. Share this site with members you know and trust. The button below opens WhatsApp with a message ready to send.
